Our Journey

With over 10 years of experience in software testing and quality assurance, Shikha Maam has been at the forefront of testing education and industry innovation.

Starting as a manual tester in 2012, she quickly mastered automation testing, API testing, and performance testing. Her passion for teaching and sharing knowledge led to the establishment of this academy in 2020.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry requirements by providing practical, hands-on training that prepares students for real-world challenges in software testing.
