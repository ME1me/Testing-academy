Welcome to Testing by Shikha Maam

Testing by Shikha Maam is a premier training platform dedicated to providing high-quality software testing education through both classroom and online modes. Based in Pune and accessible across India, our programs are designed to equip students with real-world testing skills that meet current industry standards. Led by Shikha Maam, an expert with over 10 years of experience in software testing—including manual, automation, API, and performance testing—we focus on hands-on, practical training that prepares you for real-time challenges. Whether you are a fresher looking to start a career or a professional aiming to upgrade your skills, Testing by Shikha Maam is your trusted destination for industry-relevant training, personal guidance, and career-oriented support. Our mission is to bridge the gap between academic learning and IT industry expectations with job-ready training and placement assistance.
